About Me

Born and rasied in Upstate New York, I long to escape the southwest to return to the east coast.

The last 10 years I've spent working in higher education where I've been a jack of all trades including: writer, trainer, front-end web developer, usability and accessibility tester. Now I'm looking to get back to my roots of coding. Lots has changed and I'm ready for the challenge. Bring. It. On.

When I'm not learning new technologies you can find me outside biking, hiking with my dog, knitting or attempting to play the ukulele. It's a fun life.
